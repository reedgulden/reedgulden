About Me

Hi! I’m Reed, a visual designer drawn to creating experimental, whimsical, and occasionally comical experiences through graphic design and hands-on, physical mediums.

I specialize in bold, colorful, and unexpected design solutions that blur the line between art and function. While my focus is primarily digital, I work across a wide range of media, including painting, drawing, cyanotype printing, and sculpture/ceramics. From branding and web design to experimental typography, I aim to make work that’s just as enjoyable to create as it is to experience.

When I’m not creating, I’m a Visual Arts student at Fordham University, pursuing a minor in New Media and Digital Design. Alongside my studies, I work as a teaching artist with Spark NYC, where I lead after-school programs and private lessons for students in grades K–5. Through these classes, I help young artists explore different creative mediums, build confidence, and discover their own artistic voices—an experience that meaningfully complements my own creative practice.
